# Qeep — Support

Welcome to Qeep support. Qeep is a personal QR code manager that lets you extract, save, and display QR codes from photos, PDF files, and your camera.

---

## Getting Started

### Scanning with your camera
1. Tap the **+** button to add a new entry.
2. Choose **Camera** and point it at any QR code.
3. Qeep detects the code automatically — no need to tap.
4. Give the entry a name and save it.

### Importing from your photo library
1. Tap the **+** button and choose **Photos**.
2. Select one or more photos that contain QR codes.
3. Qeep extracts all QR codes it finds.
4. Give the entry a name and save it.

### Importing a PDF or image file
1. Open Qeep from another app (e.g. Files, Mail) using the share sheet and selecting **Open in Qeep**.
2. Qeep scans the file and extracts any QR codes it finds.
3. Give the entry a name and save it.

### Displaying a saved QR code
Tap any saved entry to view its QR codes. The screen brightness is temporarily increased for easier scanning. Tap a code's text to copy it.

### Deleting an entry
Swipe left on any entry in the list and tap **Delete**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does Qeep require an internet connection?**
No. Qeep works entirely offline. All your data stays on your device.

**Where is my data stored?**
All saved QR codes are stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server or synced to the cloud.

**Why does Qeep need camera access?**
Camera access is used only to scan QR codes in real time. No photos or video are stored by the App.

**Why does Qeep need photo library access?**
Photo library access is used only to read photos you select for QR code scanning. Your photos are never stored or transmitted by the App.

**Can Qeep read QR codes from PDF files?**
Yes. You can open PDF files directly in Qeep from the Files app or any app that supports sharing. Qeep will scan every page for QR codes.

**What happens if no QR code is found in my photo or PDF?**
Qeep will let you know that no QR codes were detected in the selected source.

**How do I delete all my data?**
Delete individual entries by swiping left in the list. To remove all data at once, delete and reinstall the App.
